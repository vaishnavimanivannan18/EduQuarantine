About US

Bringing Education to YOU

EduQuarantine aims to inspire and motivate students by creating a universal classroom which transcends social, political and cultural boundaries; fostering an international community.

We aim to bring students together to bridge the social, developmental and educational gaps that have been brought about by the pandemic by providing peer to peer tutoring sessions, online resources to find support in others to face the challenges of procrastination, demotivation and online learning.
